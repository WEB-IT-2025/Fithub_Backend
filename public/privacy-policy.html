Privacy Policy - Fithub

Last updated:

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our fitness tracking service:

  • Google Account: Email, name, and profile picture for authentication
  • Google Fitness Data: Daily step counts to track your pet's health
  • GitHub Account: Username and contribution data to determine your pet's size

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Pet health calculation based on daily steps
  • Pet size determination based on GitHub contributions
  • User authentication and account management

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request data deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
